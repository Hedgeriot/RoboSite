Robotics Club is more than just robots.

Workshops

Every now and then, Robotics Club will do workshops on important skills, related to robotics or not. They're open to the school, so anybody can come! We suggest you swing by, as they teach valuable skills, and more often than not you'll get something fun!

Soldering Workshop

Our first workshop for the year is on soldering. Soldering's the use of melted metal to join wires, connectors, and other metal devices. It's commonly used in building computers, DIY projects, and fixing parts. We'll also be using this as a foundation for several of our other workshops throughout the year, such as build-your-own-keyboard.

We just finished our first soldering workshop, on September 30th. If you missed it, fret not! We are planning to have more soldering workshops throughout the year, for both beginners and returning experts.

Build Your Own Keyboard

One of our planned workshops is "Build Your Own Keyboard". Using Arduino Nanos, Mechanical Switches, Soldering, and some knowledge, we'll make some fully functional mini-keyboards!

We haven't decided on a date, and unlike other workshops, this will require pre-sign up, and possibly an entry fee. If you'd like to help out, feel free to contact Matthew Wang.
